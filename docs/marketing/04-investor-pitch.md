### Common Questions & Answers

**"Why won't Dropbox just copy this?"**
> Their business model requires storing files - that's how they make money. P2P cannabilizes their core revenue. Also, they're a public company optimizing for margins, not disrupting themselves.

**"Why not just use Syncthing?"**
> Syncthing is great for technical users, but setup takes 15+ steps. We're building for creative professionals who want one-click simplicity. Different market, different product.

**"How do you make money if it's free?"**
> Freemium model proven by Slack, GitHub, Notion. Free tier drives adoption and viral growth. Teams and enterprises pay for admin controls, compliance, and support.

**"What's your unfair advantage?"**
> 1) First to market with mount + simple + free combination. 2) Open source creates trust with privacy-conscious users. 3) Built-in virality - every share requires recipient to install.

**"What's the biggest risk?"**
> Adoption speed. We need to reach critical mass before a well-funded competitor emerges. Mitigation: Focus on creative professionals where pain is highest and decision cycles are fast.
